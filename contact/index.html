Contact

For in-game support, we need to know your Minecraft username, so make sure to include that.

If you donated through Ko-fi or bought something in the online store and need support related to that payment, please use the same email address as used on the payment platform. If you used PayPal, it would help to include the PayPal transaction number.

You can contact us by email using the email address info+ghp@bitloco.org.

Please understand that marketing emails will be ignored.

Thank you!
